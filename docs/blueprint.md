# **App Name**: YBT Connect Pro

## Core Features:

- Subscriber Landing Page: Landing page for subscribers to send messages, feedback, suggestions, and questions.
- Freelancer Landing Page: Landing page for freelancers to apply for projects, showcasing benefits, work categories, and a submission form.
- Brand Landing Page: Landing page for brands to explore collaboration opportunities, featuring creator profile summary, audience insights, social proof, and a collaboration process overview.
- Contact Form Submission: Allow users to submit messages. Each role for uses smpt and Gmail sends to All
- Data Storage: Store form submission data from all landing pages into Firestore without user authentication.
- Lead Quality Analyzer: Use an AI tool to determine the likelihood a lead will convert to working together and order them based on highest probability of working together, increasing likelihood the creator finds qualified collaborators or partnership opportunities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#293B5F), evoking professionalism and credibility in alignment with the brand's intention to establish trust and value.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F2F5), ensuring readability and a clean, modern look while maintaining a light scheme.
- Accent color: Purple (#8047C4), chosen as it is analogous to dark blue and significantly different in both brightness and saturation, ensuring calls-to-action stand out.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a modern look and a little warmth or personality
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif similar to Didot, geometric, high contrast thin-thick lines, with an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel. Pairing PT Sans with Playfair
- Utilize Material Design icons from the React Bit library to maintain consistency and clarity across all pages.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout to present the three user options on the homepage, ensuring a clear and premium user journey.
- Incorporate subtle, animated transitions throughout the platform to enhance the user experience and add a touch of sophistication.
